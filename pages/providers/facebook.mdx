- **Facebook pages** are supported.
- **Facebook groups** are not (Facebook removed this type of permission).

## Required setup

Register an app at [developers.facebook.com](https://developers.facebook.com).

## Required environment variables

### `FACEBOOK_APP_ID`

Your app ID from the developers page.

### `FACEBOOK_APP_SECRET`

Your secret from the developers page.
